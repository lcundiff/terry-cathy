    The Hurds

    Terry and Cathy Hurd, also known by many as Nana and Papa, are one of the brightest stars in the city of Palm Bay. Anyone that gets to know this one of a kind couple won't forget them! They are extrordinarily kind, generous, and warm hearted, which makes anytime at the Hurds a one to remember!

    Any lunch or dinner at Cathy and Terry's place is one filled warm hospitality, deliciously cooked food, and of course the other members of the Hurd as well! That's because its a tradition for every Christmas to spend the afternoon with all the family at their place - hanging out and catching up!

    The Hurds can also be seen at Sydney's soccer games, Camden's softball games, or Kinsey's volleyball games too! They always make an effort to spend time with their grandkids, whether its answering random facetime calls or taking everyone on a fun vacation!
